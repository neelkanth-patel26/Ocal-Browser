   OCAL BROWSER v2.6.78-BETA | "PROJECT RESCUE" DEFINITIVE UPDATE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

This release represents a complete reconstruction of Ocal's privacy and networking 
infrastructure. "Project Rescue" resolves the most persistent challenges in 
modern browsing—location leaks, regional search engine hijacking, and security 
blockades on high-traffic multimedia platforms.

══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  1. NETWORKING: THE GLOBAL RESCUE POOL (VPN v4)
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
• Multi-Protocol Failover: 
  Transitioned from static HTTP proxies to a dynamic PAC (Proxy Auto-Config) 
  Engine. Ocal now utilizes a SOCKS5/HTTPS/SOCKS4/PROXY hierarchy for the 
  ultimate resilient connection.

• Stealth SOCKS5 Nodes: 
  Added high-anonymity SOCKS5 nodes across the US, UK, and DE. Unlike standard 
  proxies, these "Stealth" nodes bypass ISP-level throttling and avoid 
  recognition by Google Search security layers.

• Dynamic Geolocation Masking: 
  Native navigator.geolocation API mocking. When VPN is active, Ocal 
  automatically reports coordinates matching your chosen secure node (e.g., 
  San Francisco for US, London for UK).

══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  2. PRIVACY: OCAL SHIELD REBORN
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
• NCR (No Country Redirect) Force: 
  Defeat regional hijacking. Ocal now automatically intercepts and sanitizes 
  Google search attempts, forcing the "google.com/ncr" route. Your US VPN 
  session will no longer be redirected to local country domains.

• TrustedScript Bypass: 
  Reworked the AdBlocker interception lifecycle. Ocal now allows YouTube 
  internal services and ocal:// protocols to function natively, eliminating 
  "TrustedScript" policy violations and page-loading hangs.

• Regional Header Sanitation: 
  Automated forcing of Accept-Language (en-US) and X-Forwarded-For markers 
  at the network layer to ensure a 100% native regional signature.

══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  3. EXPERIENCE: THE PORTAL ENGINE
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
• Portal PiP (Picture-in-Picture): 
  Integrated a high-performance "Direct-Link" video popout engine. Enjoy 
  zero-lag PiP streaming with circular glassmorphic controls and automatic 
  video detection.

• Premium UI Polishing: 
  Enhanced the Ocal Shield popup with high-DPI 512px assets, a massive 750px 
  layout for better visibility, and ultra-smooth "Antigravity" rounded bottoms.
